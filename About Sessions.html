Image detection pipeline

A Sessions capture turns a photo of a beatmania IIDX result screen into a typed, matched play record. It runs in three stages — localize the on-screen regions with a Core ML object detector, read each region with the model best suited to its content, then parse the text into a structured result and match it against the song database.

01How a capture flows

Capture and recognition are decoupled: the photo is persisted immediately, and an actor drains the work queue one play at a time so a backlog can survive app suspension and be recovered by a background task.

  1. Capture & persist
    IIDXSessionStore.capture writes the HEIC image via IIDXSessionImageStore, inserts a play in state pending, and submits its id to the processor.
  2. Queue & drain
    IIDXSessionCaptureProcessor (an actor) appends to a serial queue and processes each play under a background activity, marking it processing.
  3. Read regions
    IIDXResultReader.detect uprights and downscales the image (≤ 2048 px), runs the detector, and reads each region — returning [DetectedRegion].
  4. Parse & match
    IIDXResultParser.parse turns the regions into an IIDXResultParse, fuzzy-matching the title against song candidates loaded from the play-data database.
  5. Commit state
    The play is updated to done, needsReview, or failed; recognized text is cached as JSON and the Live Activity / watch are refreshed.

02Stage 2 — region routing

The detector localizes a region; what reads it depends on the region's content. Pure-number fields and the stylized DJ-level graphic go to dedicated models that Vision OCR cannot read; everything else stays on OCR. _prev and _delta variants are detected but ignored by the parser, which uses the _now values.

03Models

Three Core ML models plus the system Vision text recognizer. The result detector is the pipeline core and ships with the app; the digit and rank models are loaded by bundle name at runtime, so the build never depends on their presence and the pipeline degrades gracefully when one is absent. All three were exported from the DJDX PEAK training pipeline.

04Outcome states

A play is acceptable only when a song matched, the parser's confidence reached the threshold (acceptableConfidence = 0.6), and no region was flagged recognitionFailed. Confidence is min(1, (hits + bonus) / 8), where a hit is a field read with a plausible value and the bonus rewards an internally consistent EX score (EX = 2·PGreat + Great).

done
Song matched, confidence ≥ 0.6, and every region read cleanly.
needsReview
Parsed, but unmatched, below threshold, or a region failed recognition. Surfaced for manual confirmation or reprocessing.
failed
The image was unavailable or detection threw; the error is stored on the play.
